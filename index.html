About Saucha

Judith Foti is a Board Certified Massage Therapist (since 2000), Vermont licensed Esthetician, Yoga Alliance E-RYT 500 instructor, Power Pilates certified mat instructor and Duke trained Integrative Health Coach with a BA in Interpersonal Communications.

Beyond that, Judith is passionate about sharing her knowledge and experience on how to live a simple, clean, balanced and healthy life.

Saucha is Judith’s peaceful mini-spa/studio located in a private log home in beautiful Stowe, Vermont. It is here that she provides her clients with a little retreat; a place to get some TLC, a reprieve from the stress of everyday life, a sanctuary to release and recharge.

So take a few moments to honor yourself with the gift of self care… you deserve it.
